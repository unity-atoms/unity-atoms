**Q: What will the future of Unity Atoms be?**

Unity Atoms is mostly in maintainenance mode. It won't go away soon (we're here since 2018).
But due to lack of free time and also maturity of the project, there won't be any major changes coming.

Bugs will be addressed in a timely manner, though.

Further reason is also the fundamental architecture was written to work with Unity 2018, and a lot was learned since then.
